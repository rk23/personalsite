Whetstone

This is personally my favorite project I co-made at GA. It is something that I actually look forward to using once we reach a point where we can call it an MVP. I worked on it with a friend in the class and we put a lot of time into it. One of the most interesting challenges we faced was working with the asynchrony of node.js and using a synchronous cron job, and if I had to do it over I wouldn't change anything about it. Read below for a description of what it's supposed to do.


The point is to keep you accountable for some sort of creative/personal goal you set. It's like doing those 30/60 day challenges but with more motivators to get to it. We have the option to invite friends and set a daily "incentive" price. If you don't submit proof of whatever the goal was, then you pay that incentive to the pot and it gets shared among all your friends at the end of your goal.


So, for example, you could set a goal to paint three times a week for a month with three of your friends. After painting, you take a picture and upload it to our site. It then records that submission and shows it to all the friends in your group so they can keep you accountable as well. If you were to only have submitted 2x in one week, and you had an incentive of $5/day to keep you painting, then you'd pay $5 to the pot to be split with all your friends at the end.


At the point of writing, the splash page copy hasn't been written yet. Most functionality will have to be seen by registering.
